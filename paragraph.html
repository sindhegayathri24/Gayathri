Education

What is Education?

Education is the process of facilitating learning, or the acquisition of knowledge, skills, values, and habits. It can take place in formal or informal settings, and is often guided by educators or teachers.

Types of Education

  • Formal Education
  • Informal Education
  • Non-formal Education

The Importance of Education

Education plays a crucial role in personal and societal development. It helps individuals:

  1. Develop critical thinking skills.
  2. Enhance career opportunities.
  3. Participate in community and civic life.
